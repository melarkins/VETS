V.E.T.S.

Veterans Exploring Together in the Smokies

Veterans Exploring Together in the Smokies (V.E.T.S.) provides opportunities and events for all veterans who are looking to connect with their local community and each other.

Veterans Exploring Together in the Smokies (V. E. T. S.) was established in 2022, as a way for veterans to come together and support each other and their community.


Our goal is to support our local veteran community by provideing opportunites to explore new locations in our area, support each other and to continue service to others.


We partner together with other organizations to create diverse experiences and opportunites. Our first event was held in partnership with Reference Point, who holds two Veteran Range Days each year (Nothing is more fun than sending some rounds downrange!). As we continue to develop and grow we hope to partner with more organizations to give our members opportunities to last a lifetime.


We are here to support the East Tennesse Veteran community.

Thank you for your service!
